Welcome

About me

Celeste spent the majority of her years as a dancer before finding Yoga. What began as a meditation practice to nurture her mind and overall wellbeing developed into a physical Yoga practice where Celeste found for the first time the opportunity to turn inward while moving outward. From outward performance to inward presence. This created the link between her love of movement and physicality with mental and spiritual wellbeing.

Celeste is a trained Rocket™, Ashtanga and Vinyasa teacher. All classes offer an equal balance of energy and grounding stability, a chance to hit pause on the external world and tend to yourself. Dedicating herself to the practice and the teachings of Yoga Celeste creates a blend of dynamic practices that help students develop body and breath awareness, mental and emotional resilience, and most importantly, a chance to play!

Come and be part of our wonderful community!
